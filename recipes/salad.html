Thornehedge Peach Slaw

A refreshing summer slaw that blends the flavor of peaches, savoy cabbage, red bell peppers, and pecans.
I first enjoyed this slaw at a garden party in Charleston several years ago and have served it each summer
since to the delight of family and friends.

Recipe by L M KELLY.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pecan pieces
  • 1 head savoy cabbage, sliced
  • 8 fresh peaches, pitted and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • Â½ cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons celery seed
  • 1 bunch fresh mint sprigs

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the pecan pieces, cabbage, peaches, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper. Mix in the green onions, celery seed, and mint sprigs. Stir well to combine.
  2. Serve immediately.
